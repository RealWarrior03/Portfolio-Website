Trick Counting - Point Tracker

With Trick Counting - Point Tracker you will never need to search for paper and pencil ever again to play Trick Counting.

Everything you need is on your phone.

Set up with all players and start playing. While you play, you enter predictions for each player and after a round the results.

In the end you will get a summary of the game with placements of each player and per round summaries as well.
